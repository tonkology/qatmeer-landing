How It Works

Simple, efficient process in three steps

1

Load & align

Hopper & conveyor align dates for imaging.

2

Inspect & classify

Cameras + on-edge model decide keep / rework / reject.

3

Sort & collect

Actuators send fruit to chutes; operator dashboard logs runs.
