How It Works

Simple, efficient process in three steps

1

Feed & Scan

Dates are fed into the system where high-resolution cameras capture detailed images of each date.

2

AI Analysis

Our AI analyzes each date for quality, size, ripeness, and defects in real-time with industry-leading accuracy.

3

Sort & Package

Robotic arms sort dates into designated bins based on your quality specifications and packaging requirements.
